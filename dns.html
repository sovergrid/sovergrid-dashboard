Connecting an external domain

If you registered your domain elsewhere, point a CNAME record from your domain to app.sovergrid.network. TLS is provisioned automatically via Let's Encrypt.

# DNS provider config example (Cloudflare, GoDaddy, etc.)
Type: CNAME
Name: your-subdomain
Value: app.sovergrid.network
TTL: Auto
